## The Problem Nobody Talks About

Most restoration owners think their biggest problem is lead generation. They spend thousands on LSAs, PPC, and referral networks. But the real problem is not getting leads. It is what happens after the phone rings.

Every week, the average 10 truck shop loses between **$8,000 and $15,000** in revenue from three invisible leaks:

1. **Missed after hours calls** that roll to voicemail
2. **Incomplete job files** that delay carrier payments by 30 to 60 days
3. **Manual dispatch friction** that adds 45 minutes of overhead per job

## How to Run Your Own Diagnostic

Here is the framework we use with every operator we onboard. You can run this yourself in about 20 minutes.

### Step 1: Pull Your Missed Call Report

Log into your phone system and pull every missed call from the last 30 days. Filter for calls that came in between 6:00 PM and 7:00 AM. These are your after hours losses.

> Most owners are shocked when they see this number. We regularly see 15 to 25 missed after hours calls per month for a 10 truck operation.

### Step 2: Calculate the Revenue Impact

Use this simple formula:

- **Missed calls per month** x **Your booking rate** x **Average mitigation ticket** = **Monthly leak**

For example: 20 missed calls x 40% booking rate x $5,500 average ticket = **$44,000 per month** in potential revenue walking out the door.

### Step 3: Audit Your Job File Completion Rate

Pull your last 20 closed jobs and check each one for:

- Initial moisture readings documented
- Daily monitoring logs complete
- Before and after photos for every affected area
- Equipment placement records
- Final clearance readings

If fewer than 80% of your files have all five elements, you have a documentation leak that is costing you in carrier disputes and payment delays.

### Step 4: Time Your Dispatch Cycle

From the moment a lead calls to the moment a tech is dispatched, how many minutes does it take? How many phone calls are involved?

| Metric | Industry Average | Target |
|--------|-----------------|--------|
| Time to dispatch | 45 min | Under 5 min |
| Phone calls per job | 3 to 4 | 0 (automated) |
| Job file creation | Manual, 20 min | Automatic, instant |

## What to Do With This Data

Once you have these four numbers, you have a clear picture of your operational ceiling. The fix is not hiring more people. It is installing the systems that eliminate the friction.

### The Three Systems That Plug These Leaks

1. **AI Voice Intake** that answers every call on the first ring, qualifies the loss, and creates the job file automatically
2. **Automated Dispatch** that routes the right tech to the right job without phone tag
3. **Field Documentation Guardrails** that ensure your techs cannot leave a job site without complete moisture logs and photos

These are not futuristic concepts. They are production systems running right now for operators across the country.
